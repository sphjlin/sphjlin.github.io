✦ ABOUT ME

Hi! I'm Sophia.

I'm a product designer with a background in computer science and a current master's student in UX Research & Design at the University of Michigan.

My work explores how technology can be both intuitive and inclusive, empowering people to interact with digital products effortlessly.

I've always been fascinated by how thoughtful design can bridge human intention and system logic.

With my experience in interaction design, accessibility, and AR/VR prototyping, I focus on creating experiences that are both technically sound and emotionally resonant.

What kind of designer do I want to be? One who designs with empathy and precision — crafting digital experiences that feel simple, human, and meaningful.
