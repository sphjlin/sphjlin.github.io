✦ FAQ

Have Questions?

Are you open to new opportunities?

Yes — I'm actively looking for Product Design internships, and open to early-career full-time roles too. I'm based in Ann Arbor and happy with remote, hybrid, or relocation.

What does your design process actually look like?

Honestly, it depends on the project. But I usually start by talking to people before touching Figma — I want to understand what's actually going on before I start solving. From there I move through wireframes and prototypes, test early, and keep iterating. I try not to fall in love with my first idea.

What do you bring to a team?

A CS background that makes me comfortable talking to engineers, a habit of keeping decisions documented so we don't relitigate them, and genuine care about accessibility. I can also prototype in code when Figma isn't enough to communicate an interaction.

How do you like to collaborate?

I like working with people who are direct and curious. I ask a lot of questions — not to challenge, but because I find that's usually how we get to the better answer. I'm also happy to just put my head down when that's what the moment needs.
