Have Questions?

Are you open to new opportunities?

Yes. I'm actively seeking Product Design internships and I'm open to early-career full-time roles that fit my skills and values. I'm based in Ann Arbor and comfortable with remote, hybrid, or relocation.

What's your design process?

Start with the problem and people → do just-enough research → shape ideas in wireframes → prototype and test → iterate with evidence → handoff with clear specs. I balance user needs, business goals, and technical constraints.

What strengths do you bring?

I'm a designer with a CS background who can move from research to shipped UI. I'm comfortable with Figma + design systems, care about accessibility, and can prototype with code when needed. I keep docs tidy and decisions traceable.

How do you like to work with teams?

With honesty and curiosity. I like when people bring their perspectives to the table, and I do my best to listen, connect the dots, and help us move forward together.
